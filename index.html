EOS & Traction

The operating system your business has been waiting for

The Entrepreneurial Operating System, as laid out in Gino Wickman's Traction, gives leadership teams the tools and disciplines to get every person in your organization rowing in the same direction.

The challenge is that a full EOS installation isn't always the right fit for where you are right now. That's where Adalia Advisors comes in.

Pre-Traction

Your company is ready to build the habits and disciplines of EOS, but you're not yet at the size or budget that justifies a full professional installation. We work alongside your leadership team to begin practicing the core EOS tools and rhythms — so you're building the right foundation from day one.

Post-Traction

You've completed a professional EOS installation and no longer need a full-time implementer, but you want a trusted, experienced advisor in the room to facilitate your annual and quarterly sessions and keep the system healthy long-term. That's exactly what we do.
